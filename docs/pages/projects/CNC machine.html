## My CNC Machine *** ### Motivation I am forever the tinkerer, and I love to buy tools and expand my ability to accomplish things on my own. This has sparked a small journey of mine to automate the manufacturing of small products through CNC technology. I first started by making my own design from scratch but quickly moved away from that because of the accuracy that I was able to achieve not being that great. You can see some of the initial results in the image below:
Test cut on home-made CNC machine.
This machine certainly had some drawbacks: - Poor construction tolerences - Innacurate cutting precision - Slow movements I realized that I didn't want to spend my time working on making a machine and would rather make things with my machine, so I decided to purchase a DIY kit that would solve all my problems from the homemade one. Additionally, it has more power and is better equipped to do other tasks. For example, because it has a 4th axis, I am using it to make parts for my automatic self-storage concept.
